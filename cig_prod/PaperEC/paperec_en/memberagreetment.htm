10. TRANSACTION PROCEDURE
A. Initiating a Transaction on the Service. A Transaction begins with either: (i) a Seller listing Product(s) for sale on the Exchange (an "Offer to Sell"), or (ii) a Buyer entering a request for Product(s) on the Exchange (a "Request to Buy", "Requests to Buy" and "Offers to Sell" are each referred to herein as a "Posting"). The Exchange then assigns a Posting ID# to each Posting and, if appropriate, may assign a Lot I.D. for groups of Postings. Each Posting contains the principal indexes and specifications of the concerned products or services. A Posting may or may not include a price. No Posting is binding; Seller or Buyer, as the case may be, may select among the Bids or may refuse to select any Bids in its sole discretion. After the expiration of the Bidding Period, a Posting may be withdrawn if no Bid has been accepted with respect to the Posting. Members should carefully review all information included in each Posting.

B. Bids. Buyers may make a Bid on any Offer to Sell and Sellers may make a Bid on any Request to Buy using the assigned Posting I.D. or Lot I.D., as applicable. Unless otherwise stated, all Bid amounts are in Chinese RMB or U.S. Dollars, which is determined by the Seller.

C. Binding Nature of Bids. All Bids must include specific price and quantity terms. All Bids are contractually binding on the Member making the Bid until the Bid expires (as established by the Bidder when submitting the bid) or the Bid is rejected by the other party involved in the transaction; If a Posting does not include a price, no Bid is binding unless and until accepted. A Transaction shall be deemed to have occurred when both members conclude the negoation process, mutually agree on all aspects of the transaction, including by not limited to quanity and price, and notify's PaperEC.com of said agreement. Once a Transaction has occurred between the Buyer and the Seller, the Exchange removes the Posting. At the same time, the transaction result will be stored as a historical record in the data bank of PaperEC.com; and both the Buyer and the Seller will agree that this record is qualified to be presented as a kind of material evidence in case of needing.

D. Payment, Shipping & Taxes. Once a Transaction has occurred between Buyer and Seller, PaperEC.com supplies both parties information, including, but not limited to the name, address, and other contact information for each party to the other party so as to enable the parties to complete a Transaction. The two parties will perform their own duties in accordance with the system requirements of PaperEC.com in case they apply for and accept the Service. The Seller or Buyer (as agreed upon by the parties to a Transaction) is responsible for collecting and paying all applicable taxes incurred in respect of Products sold or purchased on the Exchange and all Members specifically acknowledge that PaperEC.com shall not be responsible for collecting or remitting any taxes except upon its own income.

E. Transaction Terms. If the terms and conditions agreed upon by the parties do not otherwise specify, then if the Buyer and Seller both log into PaperEC.com's Chinese language web site, the Transaction between them shall be deemed to be a domestic transaction and shall be governed by the laws of The People's Republic of China; and if either party does not log into PaperEC.com's Chinese language web site, the Transaction shall be deemed to be a international transaction governed by the United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International Sale of Goods.

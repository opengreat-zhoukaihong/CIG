Others good stats packages
Choosing a stats package is not a easy task. It's up to you to get the one which will be more useful for your web. Package listed are my favourite one, especially the first two as they are closed to my package.
Some of them computed only daily stats (accesswatch), some are fast (written in C), others have nice graphical output. These packages have their own feeling, doing few things the others don't but as they're still in development, they become close to each other.
Have a look at those web and choose the best one (hope it will be mine ;)

Shareware means you are free to pay a fee but you don't have to. It means also that a lots of work have been done, many long nights and week-end to improve and add new users features requests.

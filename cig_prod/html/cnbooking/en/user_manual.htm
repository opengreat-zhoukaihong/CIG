How to reserve Hotels?
Online Booking
Everyone can book online after registering to be a member and filling out the booking form.

Free Long Distance Call Booking
Our domestic free long distance call is about to open, and it will be convenient for merchandisers and tourists out of Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Dalian and Hongkong to go on all kinds of booking.

Booking with branches
We have branches in above-mentioned 6 cities, you can book with these offline companies through telephone or fax.

Payment methods
The fees of the room reservations of the most hotels are commissioned to charge by our center, also there being a few hotels that could make the payment possible in the front office at their hotels.

Following ways for people to book room to choose to pay the room costs:
1.Online credit. (on the edge of open)
2.Banking the room cost in our center's bank account or transferring it to our center's bank account both OK in China and Hongkong.
3.We can dispatch people to your house to get the room cost if the city of the hotel you are living in has our branch office.
4.Permanent customers and company customers can pay weekly or monthly after living in the hotel.

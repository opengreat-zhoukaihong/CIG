Great Effect In Booking Room In CNHotel Booking Center

[Tanlingrv Report]
....The executive vice president and the general in Chinese district of China Internet Group (CIG), Yangzhenyu expressed that after the network stocks got into the low tide, the fervor of foreign capitals entering into the Chinese Internet market was really not compared with before, but the investors who had known Internet really and truly still wanted to get a thick soup from Chinese market, at the same time, CIG was continuing to look out for the right enterprises to invest.
....CIG was found in October in the last year. And that's business in chief was aimed at Chinese Internet initiation companies, providing "hatch machine" services. At the same time, for those foreign investors who are not familiar with the Chinese market, CIG will act as a partner of them to lead them into Chinese market together, helping foreign venture investment land softly. The head office of CIG was set in American "Silicon Valley", having branch offices in Shengzhen, Beijing, Shanghai, and Hongkong in China. It's an all-around Internet enterprise "hatch machine" in China.
....At present, CIG has invested and developed ten corporations relating to Internet, investing about two or three million dollars in total. Among these ten corporations, there are two having gotten the venture investment; in addition, some of them have gotten into the phrase of the deep negotiation with the foreign venture capitals.
....Yangzhenyu pointed out, in the eyes of the foreign investors, Chinese Internet market's still the most potential market. Now network stocks are depressed, and those who have been affected are just blind investors. In fact the investors who really have good understandings of Internet will never abandon Chinese market. These investors don't want to invest no money, but for their saner considerations, requiring the enterprises could have the concrete executable payoff modes.
....Yangzhenyu denoted, among the Internet enterprises CIG had chosen to invest, Websites that had no definite payoff modes or just had contents, were none. He thought, in terms of the present development of China Internet, "Mouse and Concrete" (Internet combined with the traditional business running) is still one of the best modes.
....For example one of the members of CIG, CNHotel Booking Center is the typical mode of "Mouse and Concrete". Though at home the conditions of the transaction in the website had not formed, the information in the website had great propagandist effect, which as a result created a special phenomenon, "browse on line and transaction off line". On the one hand, CNHotel Booking Center filled in the information in the website, on the other hand, it developed the offline operating network. It had become one of the most capable sales domestic booking offices, its turnover being more than three million yuan monthly. Compared with those network companies just talking about concepts, CNHotel Booking Center with the support of concrete outstanding achievements of course will stand at an advantage in getting the venture investment.

[Original printing in China Business and Trade, Hongkong Economy Daily in 13th, Oct., 2000]

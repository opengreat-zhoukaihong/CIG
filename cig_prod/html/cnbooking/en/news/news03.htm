CNbooking.com Specializing in Hotel Booking

....CNbooking.com, the expert booking website starts up
....Established by the Hong Kong company, CNHoteling.com is a business tour website calling together the technology and capital from American Silicon Valley and the talents in Mainland hotel marketing industry.¡¡
....At present, it offers a list of over 1000 hotels over 3-star standard in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao. Compared with other tour website, it features not only the characteristics of complete hotels and favorable rates, but also the operating strategy of adopting both Electronic Commerce and Telephone Commerce. Now it is specialized in services of booking air-tickets and hotels of Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao, later its services will extend to omnidirectional scheme of business tour, and the booking items to be added include hotel apartment, automobile, Golf, short city trips, conference arranging, and temporary secretary, interpreter and tour guide.

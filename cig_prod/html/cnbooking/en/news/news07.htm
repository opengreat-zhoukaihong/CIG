¡°Two Legs¡± must go with E-tourism

CNbooking.com CEO Fungmukching
....www.cnbooking.com is a business trip website offering hotel booking services mainly in the grand China area including Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao. And it carries on the policy of "walking with two legs".
....The two legs of CNHotel Booking Center are respectively E-commerce and Telephone-commerce. The latter refers to its extensive offline business network. Apart from a business travel website specialized in hotel booking, it also set offline companies respectively in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Dalian and Hong Kong. While promoting E-business, take full use of telephone business as an important means of marketing. In fact, the operating strategy combining E-commerce and T-commerce is the E-commerce pattern of integrating "mouse" and "concrete" that is widely recognized in American venture capital line. Before this kind of pattern was recognized as a mainstream cognition in American Venture Capital line, CNbooking.com has extended its offline company network to offer powerful assistance for E-commerce in many aspects such as rates information, outfit services and so on.
....We believe, E-commerce will eventually take the place of Telephone-commerce in tourism industry by and by, however, at present and in the near future, E-commerce will still have great conditionality while telephone commerce will still have great vitality. Now there are many problems existing in the aspects of payment and mutual communication. As to the payment to foreign guests, if the website company sets agency in America and the balance account of online credit card is opened in New York, a lot of trouble will be saved, however, instead, if it is in Hong Kong, then the balance cycle, and the cost of execution and finance are too heavy. In the other hand, because of the lack of popularity of credit cards, the problem of online payment of domestic guests makes little sense.
....Though there is no distribution problem in the E-tourism, the problem of timely mutual communication restricts the online booking. It is the usual case that people will select carefully when they decide to take a package tour and study every tour agency no less than 15 minutes. However in detail the itinerary in the presswork is, the consumers will still have a lot of questions to ask. So before the popularization of the video system of telecommunication, we can hardly imagine online booking completely without the assistance of telephone. Because of the problems in the mutual communication, at present many businessmen and tourists choose the booking pattern of "Online browsing, offline transacting".
....CNbooking.com is an expert booking website combining the traditional booking agency and American Internet investment Company. At first when the developing strategy of adopting both E-commerce and Telephone Commerce was just set down, even the American shareholders didn¡¯t understand. At that time most of the tour websites established on venture capital was extending E-commerce, but now the situation changes, some famous websites have realized the importance of telephone commerce.

"Consultant"of Business Travel

....CNHotel Booking Center was founded in Gunagzhou 3 years ago. At that time the tourism market and group tour industry in Mainland China has flourished but lacks expert hotel booking agencies for business trip. Our Center is a booking agency specialized in introducing hotels over 3-star standard in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao. There is a selection of more than 1000 hotels to be booked in our center. Its development in Guangzhou and Shenzhen market has led a number of medium and small booking agency. Now in Gongzhou, no less than ten booking centers learn from the business pattern of CNHotel Booking Center.
....While CNHotel Booking Center invigorated the whole booking industry, it at the same time improved itself rapidly. Now we have set branches in Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Beijing, Shanghai, Dalian and Hong Kong, and the establishment of offline business network will speed up. We plan to found new branches in at least 15 main cities in China at the end of this year. At present, our turnover of independent guests booking has exceeded RMB 5,000,000 yuan and this figure is predicted to double this time next year. At that time, apart from booking, the profit will come from a variety of outfit services of business trip.
....While establishing a powerful offline network, we never slow down the speed of developing E-commerce. As early as last October, we have started up online booking service and founded the B2C E-commerce website. At present, we are establishing an online booking website integrating B2C and B2B functions in cooperation with China Internet Group Inc. (CIG) which is committed to investing in China Internet Market. The new website is committed to promoting the development of booking service and at the same time will extend the service scope to business trip outfit items such as internal and international special offer air tickets, vehicle renting, apartment, meeting planning, business visa, and providing temporary secretaries and interpreters. We will offer perfect full-set business tourism project for enterprises and independent guests.
....Our present business pattern can be described by an old Chinese saying, that is, "walking with two legs". One leg refers to the expanding offline business network and the other leg is extending to the whole world to seek strategic partners and the network providing shortcut transaction forms. The striding "two legs" are cooperating and supporting each other.
....In the next two or three years, we will greatly expand our offline operating network. If we acquire more support in the capital market, we will gain negotiation power, and thereby form our absolute advantage in the sales prices.
....We believe that the sales mode of Chinese hotel sales market will unavoidably trend to accord with overseas, so there is much space for expert booking agencies to develop. In the near future, agency like CNHotel that possesses both powerful website and extensive business operating companies will enjoy more market share.

(Origin from China Economy Guide of July 14, 2000)

The Mainland Net Businesses strings foreign financing

Hongkong Wen Wei Po Reporter Ao Pak Chao Interview Coo
....With respect to the newly issued Internet Information Service Administration Measures, there is a popular view in the trade that because some relative regulations focus on the foreign and financing activities, this shows Mainland intend to shrink the business of websites. However, at present, the mainland net merchants that don' t involved in these activities will still have a lot of room to develop.
....Lanyan, the COO (Chief Operating Officer) of CNbooking.com, points out that the Measures mainly focus on the foreign businesses and financing activities, it will not produce the mainland net merchants that are in operation and not so eager to come into the market and cooperate with foreign capitals and businesses.
....She remarks though cannot exclude the possibility that Mainland intends to demolish some cooperation projects made by Mainland net merchants and foreign capitals. However, since there was no relevant case before, and at the same time, there are many world famous consortiums investing Mainland Companies earlier, if the authorities now demolish some projects according to the Measures, it will produce great influence and pay a price, so we believe the Measures are only a routine registration procedure.
....Lanyan said, in fact, the Mainland administration of Internet mainly focuses on contents, as long as the net merchants do not exceed the bounds in the aspects of contents and ideology, most of them have great freedom of development. For example, most of today¡¯s tourism Websites are actually not approved by National Tourism Bureau while only get the commercial licenses, however, at present National Tourism Bureau has no objections towards these tourism Websites.
....She said the company will make clear if the Methods is a routine registration procedure and at the same time will make decision if submit the cooperation with foreign businesses according to the complication degree of the registration procedure.
....The speaker of Net Big also points out that the company is having the Measures researched by Chinese counselor and trying to find out if it needs to declare the past subscribe projects in Mainland China.
....He points out that the Mainland websites should declare the cooperation projects with foreign businesses and because the process of examination and approval will slow down the development of the cooperation projects, so it will more or less affect both the net merchants and foreign businesses. But relative regulations will make clear some cooperation boundary lines, so it is not necessarily a bad thing for the two parties of the cooperation.
....However, he said, because the Measures mainly aim at domestic capital net merchants, the company still wait for the authorities.
....And the report of Meilin Securities points out that because any purchases related to Internet contents suppliers, must apply for the Information Industry Department, the new Measures will slow down the paces of this kind of purchases.

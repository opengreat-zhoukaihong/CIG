Chance and condition of the room reservations in the website

CNbooking.com CEO Fungmukching
....Though it's a traditional part of business travel industries in hongkong-macau and occident, hotel room reservations in china still belongs to the burgeoning service and mesomerism line, which has been known by more people for two and three years recently. Except four large cities, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, it has not developed rapidly and generally in China, but in great contrast with this, expert room reservations website or the travel website that's mainly market room and air ticket booking jumped out more than hundreds in the last year, for example, as Shenzhen to be an operation base, WWW.Cnbooking.com is one of them, but the different is that it's a seldom room reservations website with the strong supports of offline business networks.
....In the past one year, So many companies built room reservations website, which is strong relative to easily make hotel room reservations business run in the way of E-business. Though it's a choke point and big puzzle for E-business, the ration does not exist in the room reservations website. Some years ago, Hotel reservations need to go to the travel agency for a room living form, but at present customers booking the room by the phone or the network, can live in the hotel through telling their names in the reception desk. Though hotel reservations with E-business is the easiest operational sector in travel industries, now so for as the domestic business circumstances are concerned, there remains some conditional factors to be settled.
....Payment on line is the urgent problem to be settled. It's rather easy to settle the payment problem for customer abroad only if the website open the credit card on line payment system in HK or US. ....Room reservations on line can't settle the problem of the conversation between the customer and the operating clerk, which is another condition. Though "hotel reservations" is a relatively simple matter, but customers still have questions. For example, marked price is weekend or not? Seascape or park scene? Main building or not? Service charge, state tax included? Breakfast included and so on? Though these problems are not big, inferior by comparison, E-business is worse than phone business in this point, because by phone to book room we can settle the bidirection conversation problem. E-business in this point y need to not only settle technical problems but also settle the technology using costs.Now CNhotel booking center adopted an applied way of increasing phone business as auxiliary. The six companies we set in Bejing, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Dalian and Hongkong in fact constructed a convenient phone business network for local businessman. The indeterminacy of the room price information also conditioned the room reservations with E-business. In general, expert room reservations running mainly in "Grand China" will record 1000-1500 above three stars hotels' prices and other information. These hotels' room booking prices are not static and also not simplified. Most hotels change the contract prices only one time with the agencies monthly even quarterly. But in some areas, parts of hotels' prices change frequently especially the Hongkong hotels'. When some international exhibitions holding, there are some travel agencies fry the room price, and the prices of booking room are simply different from day to night.
....People in general think all of the room reservations agencies make contracts with the hotels directly. In fact "room reservations" exists a invisible wholesale market. For example in the room reservations business of Cnbooking.com, about forty percent businesses wholesale the same trade by network or not. There are different coming prices in a hotel. Sometimes the best price is not the contract price signed with the room reservations agencies. Then why are some hotels' prices offered by some famous travel websites in the country so high? The reason is that the coming prices of them are simple, they just using self-fixing prices. Because there are so many complex and odd prices information in the hotel room reservations lines, the management of the prices information in the website becomes very difficult.
....But in the long run, to settle the prices information's instability, room reservations websites should use self-fixing prices step by step all-around. Among them, the websites that have powerful sales will get the best contract prices, and most of the small websites will be washed out by the market for they can not get the good contract prices.

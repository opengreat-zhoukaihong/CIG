Cnbooking.com Running Well

[Dove Press Agency, 18th, Report]
....Though many network stocks belonging to the initiation edition inflicted heavy losses not only in NASDAQ but also in Hongkong since March this year, in the late starting Chinese Mainland, Internet air was not half affected. All kinds of websites remained springing up like mushrooms. Like American, among them, traveling websites hold a large scale. Recently, a protrusive website, CNbooking.com was just one of them. It was founded by China Internet Investment Corporation whose head office is located in American "Silicon Valley", Hongkong traditional expert hotel reservation agencies, and some sales talents from domestic tourism. In the same websites in China, shareholders assembling could be rather special.
....Website CEO Lanyan expressed to the reporters that since international venture investment corporations in recent months adjusted their investment directions, with the addition of many a traveling website appearing suddenly in China, at present the competition in the fields of survival and requirement of the venture investment was very intensive. Of them, "Pure Website" was in a pickle; except the few that had gotten the venture investment before March of this year, the most of them run badly. Owing to the lagging of the payment procedures and the shopping habits on line in the Chinese mainland, "Pure Website" was so hard to get the turnover that could support their daily running. But she revealed, the business of CNHotel Booking Center is running well, and it's the handful one of the reservation networks with no capital pressure before the venture investment coming out, and the reason is that's unusual operating mode.
....She explained, as long ago as the former half of this year, websites was still in construction. The decision-making classes of the three joint venture parts, China, Hongkong, United states had realized that there would be staged conditional factors if generalizing E-business roundly in China. So while building their website, they made their offline operating companies that they had have increase from two to six. But at that time, the E-business mode of "concrete and mouse" still didn't become the prevalent cognition. Up to now, CNHotel Booking center has built the offline operating networks in Hongkong, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen and Dalian, six chief economic cities. When the website came out formally in July, and after having been generalized step by step, it brought considerable turnover for the offline operating companies, at the same time offline networks offered great aid for the website in the fields of the price information and customers supports. Lanyan said this kind of development mode would become their staged operating strategy, and that could be the Chinese E-business staged development strategy too. But she also pointed out as time went on, E-business in China would give off its powerful life gradually.
....Lanyan denoted, the reason for hundreds of travel and business reservation networks issuing out in the former half of this year in Chinese mainland related to this line's great market potential and the bottleneck limitation without ration, but because the "pure website" itself conditions a lot, plus be hard to get enough venture investment, in about half an year, most of them would end or go in a state of half dormancy, except those who had large offline operating networks to support would have powerful lives for the profit of the offline companies was enough to make the website work well. Still and all, not only the "pure website" but also the "concrete and mouse" website, without venture investment in a large scale, would be difficult to have great development.

Original printing in North American Dove Press Agency Network in 18th, Oct., 2000

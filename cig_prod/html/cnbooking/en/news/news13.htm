Walking by two legs, CNbooking.com has made profit

....Feng Muk Ching, CEO of CNbooking.com, made speech in the China Internet Business Initiation and Venture Capital Investment Conference and answered the press gang of the questions about the working ideas of CNbooking and the E-commerce process of China business travel.
....Now there is a popular saying that there will be no Internet companies 5 years later, because all the companies will enter into Internet. Feng agrees with this kind of saying, but he remarks this process will undertake 8 or 10 years in China. He also predicts that telephone commerce will still works prior to E-commerce in the next two years in the Chinese market of tourism, hotel-booking and air-ticket booking. And two years after that online booking will parallel with telephone booking and by and by will take the place of telephone commerce.
....Based on this judgement, CNbooking.com will greatly extend the offline network in the next three years. If it can acquire the further support of capital market, CNbooking.com will found branches in 20 developed cities in China. After such kind of network is established, it will undoubtedly provide great support for the website. ....Compared with those developed countries, the business travel and hotel booking industry is now obviously in its elementary stage. However, Feng Muk Ching holds the view that it is just the chance for CNbooking.com, and he also discloses that the booking turnover of independent guest has amounted to RMB 5,000,000 yuan, and this figure is predicted to double in the next year. The facts show that CNbooking is making profits.

CNbooking.com, the expert booking website starts up

....CNbooking.com, with a good fame of booking expert in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao, started up formally in last month. It is the only expert booking website in Mainland China and Hong Kong district with the powerful assistance of offline companies and richest resources of hotels in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao.
....Founded by Hong Kong company, CNbooking.com is a business tourism website combining the technology and capital from American Silicon Valley and hotel marketing talents in Mainland. The American investor CIG Corporation, headquartered in Silicon Valley, is the hatcher of China Internet while The Hong Kong Corporation CN Booking Center is an expert hotel-booking agency with many offline branches in Hong Kong, Beijing, Shnaghai, Shenzhen, Guangzhou and Dalian.
....Now there are more than 1000 hotels above 3-star standard in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao that are registered in the website, so among the five big hotel booking websites it features the most complete hotels and most favorable rates. Room rates offered by it of some domestic hotels are 20% to 30% less than average travel agencies in Hong Kong while number and rates of Hong Kong hotels offered by CNbooking.com are also very preferential.
....Compared with other travel booking websites, CNbooking.com adopts the operating strategies of both telephone commerce and E-commerce. Businessman and tourists can not only book online but also make a reservation through telephone after browsing the attractive room rates in the website.
....At present, CNbooking.com is specialized in hotel and air-ticket booking in Mainland China, Hong Kong and Macao, later, its service scope will extend to a whole set of business trip items and the business booking service items to be added include hotel apartments, vehicles, Golf, short city trips, conference arrangement and the service of temporary secretary, interpreter and tour guide.
....CNbooking.com is now planning to induct the second venture capital and aiming to develop into the biggest B2C business travel website and one of the three big hotel wholesale agencies (B2B).

(Origin from Net Version, Investment Guide Paper, July 21, 2000)

Company Profile
Founded in April 2000, PaperEC.com is a vertical B2B full solution provider for China's pulp and paper industry, and provides a true marketplace for the online transaction of pulp and paper products. Overseas experts with long Internet and management history, as well as domestic paper organizations and scholars founded the company.

Industry Background
The paper industry is one of the basic industries of every national economy. It is closely connected with society, the economy and people's lives. The pulp and paper industry is also closely related to news, press, printing, packing and forestry. The consumption of paper products in China has increased greatly in recent times. For instance, Chinese domestic consumption of paper and paper products in 1999 was 33.47 million tons, representing a value of USD 32 billion, of which, nearly USD 6 billion were imported paper products.

The Chinese pulp and paper market comprises approximately 75,000 companies; of which approximately half are paper producers and the other half use paper as a raw material. Of China's paper producers, nearly 98% are small-scale companies and are scattered throughout the country. Small-scale paper product producers in China, when compared to the larger enterprises, are at a distinct disadvantage with regard to expanding market share, conducting international trade, and improving operation efficiency. PaperEC.com will level the playing field.

Further, the pulp and paper industry is well situated to adopt e-Commerce. Two major factors contribute to this. One, paper is a standardized product that can be accurately described through product specifications. Two, the pulp and paper industry is a global industry and people in the industry are used to conducting transaction with other parties in remote locations.

Business Model
PaperEC.com is a professional B2B web site providing e-commerce for the online transaction of paper products. Through the trading floor, PaperEC.com enables both buyers and sellers to negotiate prices and make deals easily and directly.

(1) Posting and Negotiation
Sellers can post product offers on the web site and make these available to one, a list of their choice, or to all buyers. The same is true of buyers, they can post product inquires and send these to one, a list of choice, or to all sellers. Buyers can then respond to the offers and begin negotiations. Each supplier can respond to the inquiry by stating his conditions and price, and then the buyer can negotiate with one or several suppliers at once. The negotiation is carried out anonymously.

(2) Transaction Manager
The participants are in complete control of their information and choose what information they want to share with others, with the result that competitors will never be able to access each other's information. Participants are also fully in charge of how they sell their products (fixed price, negotiation or auction) and with whom they make deals (one player, a list of players or all). Each transaction is on an individual to individual basis.

(3) Transaction Conclusion
When both sides reach an agreement through their negotiations, the seller submits a transaction application to PaperEC.com and signs an electronic contract. The web site signs the contract and sends it to the buyer for his signature. With all three parties' signatures, the electronic contract becomes valid and the transaction is successful.

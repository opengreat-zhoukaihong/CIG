Management Team
The Management team of PaperEC.com features people with backgrounds in e-commerce, experience with China's pulp and paper industry and experience in conducting business in China. Listed below are key members of PaperEC.com.

Mr. Kevin Ding, Co-founder, President and CEO
Mr. Ding, one of the principal co-founders of the company, has extensive experience in corporate and strategic planning after holding various positions with several large foreign investment companies and state-owned enterprises. His duties have included investment planning as well as import and export planning and development. Before co-founding PaperEC.com, Mr. Ding was Executive Vice President (International Trading) for Huatai Enterprise Management Co., Ltd. Mr. Ding received his MBA in Marketing from Shanghai Jiaotong University in 1988.

Mr. Greg Ye, acting CFO
Mr. Ye is currently Vice President of China Internet Group. Before joining CIG, Mr. Ye served as a senior director at PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP, in the Global Capital Markets Group and as a senior manager for the High Tech Practice Group. In addition, Mr. Ye placed second on the 1993 American CPA examination; and, is both a CPA and CMA. He is pursuing his MBA from Harvard Business School.

Mr. Edward Yu, acting CTO
Mr. Yu has been developing Internet and telecommunication systems for over 10 years. He has worked heavily with AT&T developing systems that affect millions of people every day, including the routing management system for 800 numbers, and design and development of AT&T's WorldNet project. Mr. Yu received his B.S. in Computer Science in 1988 from Shanghai Jiaotong University, and in 1990 received his M.S. in Computer Science from the New Jersey Institute of Technology in the United States.

Ms. Ying Li, Vice President of International Trade
In 1996 Ms. Li co-founded Shandong Pentacle Trade Development Ltd., a company specializing in the import and export of paper products from China. Since that time she has served as General Manager of Pentacle. Ms. Li has over 20 years experience in international trade, including extensive experience in the assessment of quality, marketing, sales channels, and pricing of paper products. Pentacle Trade Development Ltd. is a subsidiary of PaperEC.com.

Mr. Xiushen Yang, Vice President of Products & Services
Mr. Yang was a senior engineer with Huawen Paper Co., Ltd. in Shandong province before becoming that company's Chairman and General Manager. Mr. Yang is extremely talented in scientific research, product innovation and technology development in the paper industry. He received his bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering from Shandong Institute of Metallurgy in 1983.

Mr. Ronny Hu, Director of Sales
Mr. Hu has a long history in sales, marketing and management in the Chinese papermaking industry. He received his bachelor degree in 1989 from Xiamen University. In 1998 he received his masters degree in Business and Economics from the China Academy of Social Science.

Jaakko Pöyry Consulting
Jaakko Pöyry Group was founded in 1958 in Finland. Following four decades of successful international project work and systematic expansion and diversification of operations, the Group today conducts its operations in three business areas (forest industry, energy, infrastructure and environment) through offices in more than 30 countries around the globe and employs 4,300 people. Jaakko Pöyry Consulting is an independent management consulting company within the Jaakko Pöyry Group and is recognized as the world's leading advisor to the global forest industry cluster. The Company concentrates on identifying, analyzing, and implementing creative business solutions designed to enhance shareholder value and create winning companies.

In Europe, PaperX.com is the sole partner of Jaakko Pöyry in the paper e-commerce field. In the Greater China region, Jaakko Pöyry has established an exclusive online service center and exclusive cooperative relations with PaperEC.com.

Jaakko Pöyry Consulting's operations are divided into four companies. JP Management Consulting gives strategic advisory services and covers the complete value chain for the industry providing expertise within Fiber Resources and Wood Products, Marketing and Business Strategies, Technology and Mill Operations, as well as Corporate Finance and Investment Analysis. JP Capital International provides professional corporate finance services, JP Operations Management concentrates on implementation advice and on-site operations support, and JP Development works with governmental and institutional organizations in the sustainable management of natural resources.

For more information on Jaakko Pöyry Consulting, visit the company at www.consulting.poyry.com

People's Daily (www. Peopledaily. com.cn)
The People's Daily is the most authoritative newspaper in China. Launched in June 1948 and with current circulation of 3 million, the People's Daily is one of China's highest circulating dailies and, according to UNESCO, ranks as one of the world's Big Ten newspapers. The People's Daily is headquartered in Beijing, maintains offices in Hong Kong and Macao, and has 37 correspondent bureaus throughout China's provinces, municipalities, autonomous regions and economic special zones. Its 32 overseas bureaus can be found in the capitals of countries across five continents.

The People's Daily has agreed to provide marketing campaign and promotional services for PaperEC.com. Furthermore, PaperEC.com will be the preferred vendor of the People's Daily for e-commerce procurement of paper products. Additional, the People's Daily will recommend use of PaperEC.com to other companies throughout China.

COSCO
PaperEC.com has just recently entered into a strategic alliance with COSCO, China's largest ocean shipping company, and the world's fourth largest shipping company. PaperEC.com will use COSCO as the preferred shipper for goods transacted through use of the web site. In return, COSCO will provide PaperEC.com members favorable shipping terms and prices at a significant discount. In addition, PaperEC.com and COSCO will jointly develop 3rd party logistic capability for service companies involved in e-Commerce activities in the paper and pulp industries of China

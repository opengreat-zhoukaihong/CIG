Huatai Enterprises Management Co., Ltd.
Huatai Co. Ltd. is an individual limited company held by People's Daily Agency, together with five other Chinese enterprises.

Taking advantage of powerful incorporeal capital and human resources of the People's Daily Agency, Huatai has developed very quickly due to recent acquisitions of companies involved in the industries of electronic information, media, pulp and paper, as well as printing. Huatai owns and operates four paper mills and two printing plants with the total output of 150,000 MT/year in Shandong province. Additionally, Huatai owns a computer network security company in China. The management personnel of Huatai are among China's best pulp and paper managers. At present, the net capital of Huatai is over 200 million RMB.

Huatai has a long history of producing quality paper goods, and brings with it a large supply of customers (over 2,000) from Shangdong province. The company has over 70 sales offices throughout China.

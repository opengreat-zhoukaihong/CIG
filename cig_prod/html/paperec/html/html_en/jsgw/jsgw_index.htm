Operations Management
Operations Management provides implementation advice and on-site operations support to companies in the pulp and paper industry in order to increase efficiency and profitability. The Company takes a hands-on approach to operational improvement issues, supporting management with concrete solutions and providing a team to help them.
In addition to its own management and operations expertise, the Company utilises the resources of the forest industry and forest industry consulting business groups to effectively cover a wide range of customer specific issues.

Development Management
Development Management works with governments, sector organisations, communities, financing agencies and other stakeholders in the sustainable management of natural resources.

The Company focuses on addressing a broad range of social, environmental and policy issues to ensure the effective integration of forestry development on social issues. Key focus areas include ecosystem protection, biodiversity conservation and environmental impact analysis.

The Company also focuses on policy formulation and institutional building services for governmental and supranational organisations involved in the natural resource sector.
Capital International
Capital International provides professional corporate finance services to clients within the Forestry and Forest Products industry cluster. The Company combines the best practises in investment banking with leading edge industry expertise. This combination enables JP Capital International to provide tailor-made investment advice focused specifically on issues in the industry.
The Company's objectives are to provide the best possible technical and financial analysis, experience based transactional strategic advice, and comprehensive execution to corporate clients, governments, venture capital firms as well as other less focused investment banks. Services include execution and advice on acquisitions, divestitures, restructuring, capital structure, and funding.

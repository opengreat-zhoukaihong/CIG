With My WatchList, you receive automatic notification of new postings of any products you choose.

  • When you submit a new posting on PaperEC, that product will automatically be added to you're my WatchList.
  • If you wish to add a product to your My WatchList while viewing any posting, click on "Add to My WatchList".
  • To view the entire posting for any product listed in My WatchList, just click on the posting.
